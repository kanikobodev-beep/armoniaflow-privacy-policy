Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 05, 2025

Information Collected and How It Is Used

This app uses the user's device's built-in pedometer sensor and accelerometer to detect the user's steps and physical activity status (e.g., walking, jogging, etc.).

Collected step count and motion data is stored locally on the device solely for the purpose of app functionality (automatic music theme switching, step count display).

Information Sharing

Collected data is not shared with any third parties or transmitted to external servers.

Permissions

The "Physical Activity" permission is required to use the app's features. This permission is used solely to accurately count steps and recognize physical activity status.
